Privacy Policy for MeetOps

Effective Date: April 2025

Your privacy is our default. MeetOps stores everything locally on your device. No accounts. No cloud sync. No data sold.

1. Data We Collect

MeetOps does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information you enter — including your class schedule, meeting links, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage. We do not have access to this data. It never leaves your device.

2. Accessibility Service

MeetOps uses Android's Accessibility Service to automatically navigate the Microsoft Teams interface and join scheduled meetings on your behalf.

Specifically, the service:

  • Detects when a scheduled meeting begins.
  • Taps the "Join" button within Teams.
  • Turns off your microphone and camera before joining.
  • Stops all automation as soon as you join.

The Accessibility Service does NOT read your personal messages, contacts, chat history, or any other screen content. It only interacts with meeting join controls. This service is entirely optional and can be disabled from Settings at any time.

3. Alarm & Audio

When the "Full Volume Alarm" option is enabled for a class, MeetOps will override your device's silent or vibrate mode and play an alarm at maximum volume at the scheduled class time.

This behaviour is disclosed to you before you enable this feature and requires your explicit confirmation. You can disable it at any time by editing the class.

4. Permissions Used

  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: Fires precisely at your class start time.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Reschedules alarms after a device reboot.
  • WAKE_LOCK: Wakes the screen momentarily to launch the meeting app.
  • USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT: Shows the alarm over the lock screen.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Delivers the "I'm awake" alarm notification.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Keeps the alarm ringing reliably in the background.
  • Accessibility Service: Automates Teams meeting join — optional, never reads messages.

5. Third-Party Services

MeetOps integrates with the following third-party SDKs that may collect data independently under their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat — Processes in-app purchases and subscription status. (See revenuecat.com/privacy)
  • Google AdMob — Serves advertisements on the free tier. (See policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Google Play Billing — Handles payment processing for in-app purchases.

6. Data Retention

All data stored by MeetOps lives on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data permanently. We retain no copies on any server.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notice. Continued use of MeetOps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
